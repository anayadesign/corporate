BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Joshua Chodniewicz

Board Member

Joshua is one of the co-founders of Art.com Inc., and currently serves on its Board of Directors. He has in-depth knowledge of the Internet, e-commerce and art industries, and has spent the last 11 years growing the company, and providing it with vision and direction.

His greatest accomplishment has been building Art.com Inc. into the world's largest online consumer marketplace for prints, posters, photographs, and custom frames. He is a firm believer that great companies are made by great people, and attributes much of Art.com Inc.'s success to its employees.

In addition to his business responsibilities, Joshua also enjoys sharing his passion for creating new businesses with high school and college students interested in becoming entrepreneurs.

Joshua attended Rutgers University in New Jersey.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Bob Kagle

BOARD MEMBER

With over 20 years of venture capital investment experience, Bob is a veteran of the venture industry and is currently a general partner with Benchmark Capital. Prior to Benchmark, Bob spent 12 years as a general partner with Technology Venture Investors. Before TVI, Bob worked for The Boston Consulting Group where he focused on issues of corporate strategy in industries ranging from retail distribution to high technology manufacturing.

Bob earned a M.B.A. from the Stanford Graduate School of Business in 1980 as a General Motors Fellow, and a B.S. in Electrical and Mechanical Engineering from the General Motors Institute (renamed Kettering University) in 1978 as a Sobey Scholar.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Dan Marriott

Chairman of the Board

Dan has more than 17 years of operating and strategic investment experience in the technology and consumer sectors and is currently Managing Partner of Stripes Group, an operationally-oriented growth stage private equity firm, which focuses on technology enabled and consumer product companies.

Prior to Stripes Group, Dan spent 11 years at IAC/InterActiveCorp (NASDAQ: IACI), where he played a variety of executive roles including: head of strategy and corporate development of IAC, founding CEO of Pronto, Inc., President of Citysearch and head of corporate strategy and development at Ticketmaster.

Prior to IAC, Dan also worked for PepsiCo's Frito-Lay Division for 6 years where he spent time in Brand Management, New Product Development and Operations.

Dan earned both his B.S. (1990) and M.BA. (1991) from the University of Illinois, where he was a Bronze Tablet Scholar.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Sharon Mccollam

Board Member

Sharon brings strategic insight and an impressive track record in finance and operations to her role as Chairperson of the Audit Committee. She is the former Executive Vice President, Chief Operating and Chief Financial Officer at Williams-Sonoma, Inc., a $3.7 billion, multi-channel specialty retailer of high-quality products for the home. She was a member of Williams-Sonoma, Inc.'s Board of Directors from 2010 to 2012. Sharon previously served as a Director of Del Monte Foods Company from 2007 to 2011 and currently serves on the Board of Directors of Sutter Health.

Sharon received a B.S. in Accounting from the University of Central Oklahoma and is a Certified Public Accountant.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

David Quinlivan

Board Member

David is a co-founder of Saints Capital, a leading secondary investor in growth companies. David's investing experience at Saints spans 12 years and includes investments in companies in the internet, software and technology industries. Prior to joining Saints, David was a senior executive at a publicly traded e-commerce company and an investment banker focused on mergers and acquisitions at Credit Suisse First Boston. David earned an MBA from Stanford in 1995, an MS in Physics from UCLA in 1990, and a BA in Physics from Harvard in 1988.

BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Alan Spoon

Board Member

Alan has more than 20 years of operating executive and investment experience and is currently a managing general partner with Polaris Partners. Alan focuses on investments in information technology, with an emphasis on revenue-stage companies, digital media, and e-commerce.

Before joining Polaris, Alan was with The Washington Post Company, where he worked for 18 years. During his career with The Washington Post Company, he served as President, Chief Operating Officer, board member, Chief Financial Officer, President of Newsweek, head of newspaper marketing, and head of corporate business development. Prior to The Washington Post Company, Alan was an officer at The Boston Consulting Group, an international management consulting firm specializing in corporate strategy.

Alan earned his B.S. at Massachusetts Institute of Technology; an M.S. at M.I.T.'s Sloan School of Management; and a J.D., with honors, from Harvard Law School.
